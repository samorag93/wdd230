BUSINESS CREATION

The Community of Madrid has returned to the positive path of creation of new companies in the region. According to data from the National Institute of Statistics (INE), in the month of January 4.9% more jobs were created new companies, in an interannual rate, which represents up to 2,143 companies incorporated commercial companies. With these data, the region once again obtains positive figures after recording a decrease of 2.26% in December 2023.

WORLD DAY OF WOMEN IN THE SCIENCE

On the occasion of World Day of Women and Girls in Science, the Madrid Chamber of Commerce has called for greater support for women entrepreneurs and businesswomen in the field of science, technology, engineering and mathematics. In addition, it has called on public administrations and educational and business institutions to increase the presence of women in STEM environments, since only 16% of professionals in these areas are women.

LESS UNEMPLOYED IN THE LAST YEAR

In the fourth quarter of 2023, Madrid was the autonomous community that registered the largest annual decrease in unemployment, with 54,200 fewer unemployed, 13.05% less compared to the end of 2022. According to the Active Population Survey (EPA) published by the National Institute of Statistics (INE), the region reaches the figure of 361,000 unemployed, after the quarter from October to December registered a decrease in unemployment of 6.66%, with 25,800 fewer unemployed than in the previous quarter. For its part, the number of employed people increased in one year by 5.02% (+160,500) to reach 3,355,500 workers in 2023.
